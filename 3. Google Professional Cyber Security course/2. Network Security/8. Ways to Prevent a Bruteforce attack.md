1. **Limit Attempts** ( very basic) + Account logout mechanism would slow down the process and annoy threat actors. 
2. **Enforcing strong passwords** would make bruteforcing harder. 
3. **Implementing MFA** (multi factor Authentication) - even if the hacker guesses the password he can't get the access. 
4. **CAPTCHA** - after few wrong attempts a captcha would slow down and harder to automate the bruteforce. 
5. **Password Hashing & Salting** - storing the password hashed + salted in the database.
